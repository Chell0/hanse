Hanse Impex Ltd. is a manufacturing company that produces high quality home and personal care goods. We are a young and fostering company determined to build a heritage in Africa. We are highly focused on producing high quality home and personal care products. Our own brand, Ever Fresh, is already available in the majority of supermarkets in Nairobi. Our production facilities and administrative offices are located in Nairobi, Kenya, and our sales and distribution teams are spread out throughout Kenya.
