Our History

Hanse Impex Company Ltd is a private company that was incorporated in Kenya in the year 2014. The company is located at Ruaraka off Baba Dogo Road, Unicorn Go-downs in Go-down no.2, Nairobi. The company began its business with trading items. The major supplies were from Turkey and India. Then we revised our core business model and we became a full flagged manufacturing company in the year 2017 up to today. The company's major activities include manufacturing of: disinfectant toilet balls, air freshener disinfectant toilet balls, disinfectant blocks, instant hand sanitizer, bleach, dishwashing liquids, multipurpose liquids, glass & window cleaner, car shampoo, carpet shampoo, hand wash, scouring powder, dishwashing paste, dishwashing liquid, shower gel, fabric softener, air freshener, toilet cleaner. Our product is Ever Fresh and we sell directly to retailers, wholesalers and through our national distributor called Ponders Ltd We also export to Uganda, Burundi and to the entire world. Our brand has managed to penetrate the market because of its unique quality and brand appealing.
