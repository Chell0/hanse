Our History

Hanse Impex Company Ltd is a private company that was incorporated in Kenya in the year 2014. The company is located at Ruaraka off Baba Dogo Road, Unicorn Go-downs in Go-down no.2, Nairobi. The company began its business with trading items. The major supplies were from Turkey and India. Then we revised our core business model and we became a full flagged manufacturing company in the year 2017 up to today. The company's major activities include manufacturing of: Disinfectant Toilet Balls, Air Freshener, Disinfectant Liquid Detergents, Hand Sanitizer, Bleach, Dishwashing Paste & Liquid, Multipurpose Liquids, Glass & Window Cleaner, Car Shampoo and Carpet & Upholstery Shampoo, Hand Wash, Scouring Powder, Shower Gel, Fabric Softener, Air Freshener Blocks, Toilet Cleaner. Our brand is Ever Fresh and we sell directly to retailers, wholesalers and through our national distributor called Ponders Ltd. We export to parts of Africa and aim to expand globally. Our brand has managed to penetrate the market because of its unique quality and brand appeal.
