About Us

Hanse Impex Company Ltd is a manufacturing company that produces high quality home and personal care goods. We are a young and fostering company determined to build a heritage in Africa. We are highly focused on producing high quality home and personal care products. Our own brand, Ever Fresh, is already available in the majority of supermarkets in Nairobi. Our production facilities and administrative offices are located in Nairobi, Kenya, and our sales and distribution teams are spread out throughout Kenya.

Our History

Hanse Impex Company Ltd is a private company that was incorporated in Kenya in the year 2014. The company is located at Ruaraka off Baba Dogo Road, Unicorn Go-downs in Go-down no.2, Nairobi. The company began its business with trading items. The major supplies were from Turkey and India. Then we revised our core business model and we became a full flagged manufacturing company in the year 2017 up to today. The company's major activities include manufacturing of: Air Freshener, Air Freshener Blocks, Bleach, Car and Carpet & Upholstery Shampoo, Dish Washing Liquid & Paste, Disinfectant Liquid Detergents, Disinfectant Toilet Balls, Fabric Softener, Foam Soap, Hand Sanitizer, Hand Wash, Multipurpose Liquid Detergents, Scouring Powder, Shower Gel, Toilet Cleaners, and Window Cleaners. Our brand is EVER FRESH and we sell directly to retailers, wholesalers and through our national distributor called Ponders Ltd. We export to parts of Africa and aim to expand globally. Our brand has managed to penetrate the market because of its unique quality and brand appeal.
